ABOUT

The "Live Cricket Score" section of a website would provide real-time updates on ongoing cricket matches. It would likely include the following information:
The teams playing and their current scores
The current over and the remaining overs
The current bowler and batsman
The scorecard of the batting team, including the runs scored, number of boundaries, and number of wickets lost
The scorecard of the fielding team, including the runs conceded, number of wickets taken, and the economy rate of the bowlers
Option for ball by ball updates.
